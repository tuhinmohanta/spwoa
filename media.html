Post Summit CTO Community Talk • Mar 2026

After the Summit Lights Fade: Hard Decisions for India's AI Decade

In this post-summit CTO Community talk, I outline hard decisions for technology leaders shaping 12-24 month roadmaps as India's AI acceleration scales rapidly. The session frames why India's constraints - language diversity, low-connectivity environments, and cost pressure - are becoming globally exportable design patterns. I unpack the three-layer architecture: digital public infrastructure rails (Aadhaar, ABHA, Bhashini), sector execution stacks in healthcare, agriculture, and manufacturing, and the capital backbone driving R&D and semiconductor momentum. In healthcare, I discuss how SAHI and BODH raise governance and validation rigor; in education, why offline-first and voice-first are no longer optional. The talk closes with four executive calls to action: audit your portfolio now, position clearly in the ecosystem, move AI governance to board-level discipline, and go voice-first to reach the real India.

ISB Alumni Panel • Feb 2026

When Technology Meets Humanity: Inside India's AI Kumbh

I led the healthcare AI and equity architecture discussion in this pan-India ISB alumni session following India AI Impact Summit 2026. Speaking alongside Mukesh Joshi (education governance), Sailaja Raman (social impact), Harshal Jawale (manufacturing & agriculture), and Sudhir Dharan (ecosystem infrastructure), I explored how India's SAHI-BODH-ABHA stack creates the foundation for responsible AI at population scale. From ASHABot deployment to token economics linking LLM costs to ESG footprint, we tackled the essential question: how do we build AI systems for 1.4 billion people where governance and equity are architecture, not afterthought—and where those closest to the problem lead, not follow? Alumni from Hyderabad, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Delhi, Chennai, Pune and beyond joined this leadership conversation.
